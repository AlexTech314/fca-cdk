# All Copy Products Acquires Ceres Technology Group

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/all-copy-products-acquires-ceres-technology-group/

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** May 27, 2016

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately-held, middle-market companies, is pleased to announce the acquisition of Ceres Technology Group by All Copy Products. Ceres will be moving under Verticomm, the IT Division of All Copy Products.

"The team of Ceres Technology Group are experts in the business voice solutions and managed services arena and are passionate about the same thing we are â€“ giving our customers the best customer experience and all-in-one solutions as we work towards being a leader in the business technology sector in our markets," stated Brad Knepper, CEO of All Copy Products.

Ceres Technology Group has served the greater Denver area for over 20 years, joining All Copy Products' 40 years in business. This acquisition will further All Copy Products' goal to provide its customers with the best customer service and sole partner for all business technology needs in its markets.

Darrin LeBlanc, Vice President of Ceres Technology Group continued, "Ceres is excited to be joining the All Copy Products team. Our customers will now be able to receive top-notch IT services and solutions while still enjoying the Customer-Centric Managed Service they are used to from Ceres."

Flatirons Capital Advisors was the exclusive sell-side advisor for Ceres Technology Group, Inc.

## About Ceres Technology Group

Ceres Technology Group has been serving the Boulder area since 1993, providing IT Support such as technical helpdesk support, computer support, and business phone systems to small and medium-sized businesses. Their experience has allowed them to build and develop the infrastructure needed to keep prices affordable and clients up and running.

---

## About All Copy Products

All Copy Products celebrated their 40th anniversary in 2015, since opening their doors in 1975. All Copy Products has strived to be a leader in the office technology industry and has grown to be a trusted leader in digital office equipment, managed print services, workflow solutions, and document management. The IT division of All Copy Products, Verticomm is a leader in providing managed services, cloud solutions, and voice services.
