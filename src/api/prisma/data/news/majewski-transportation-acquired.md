(Majewski), headquartered in Houston, TX by River Horse Logistics, LLC headquartered in Memphis, TN.

Majewski (majewskitransportation.com), founded in 1990, is a family-owned and operated company providing seamless transportation services such as LTL, TL, consolidations, logistics, warehousing plus import/export, both air and ocean.

Managing Member Mike Allen led the Flatirons Capital Advisors deal team that advised Majewski Transportation on the transaction. "Majewski's longevity, location, diversification, solid management team, and high profile client base make it a great fit for River Horse Logistics," stated Allen.

River Horse Logistics, LLC is a holding company founded in 2015. River Horse's acquisition strategy is focused on major port and transportation hubs. Target companies will cover ports in Oakland, LA, Houston, Miami, and Newark and the markets of Memphis, and San Jose.
