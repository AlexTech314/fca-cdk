# New State Capital Takes Majority Stake In KMS

**URL:** https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/new-state-capital-takes-majority-stake-in-kms-301183459.html

**Author:** PR Newswire

**Date:** December 2, 2020

---

New State Capital Partners (New State) today announced that it has made a significant equity investment in KMS, LLC (KMS or the Company).

KMS is one of the largest wholesale resellers of closeout consumer products in the US. The Company provides a solution for brands to monetize excess or discontinued inventory.

---

## About KMS

KMS is one of the nation's largest wholesalers of consumer product closeouts. The company is a specialized wholesale reseller of closeout, overstock, and factory refurbished consumer products.

---

## About New State Capital Partners

New State Capital Partners is a private equity firm that seeks to invest in market-leading companies with $8 million to $30 million of EBITDA in the areas of business services, industrials, and consumer products.
