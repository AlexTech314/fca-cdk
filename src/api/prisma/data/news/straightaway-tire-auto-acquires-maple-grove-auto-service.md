# Straightaway Tire & Auto Acquires Maple Grove Auto Service

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/straightaway-tire-auto-acquires-maple-grove-auto-service/

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** January 2024

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately held, middle-market companies, is pleased to announce the acquisition of Maple Grove Auto Service by Straightaway Tire & Auto. Straightaway Tire & Auto, a prominent independent automotive repair and service platform, has further strengthened its presence in Minnesota through this acquisition.

Maple Grove Auto Service, a well-established full-service auto repair facility serving the Maple Grove, Minnesota market for over forty years, is now part of the Straightaway family. The acquisition reinforces Straightaway's robust position in Minnesota, with a focus on expanding its service offerings and customer reach.

Joe Vallee at O2 Investment Partners, the private equity group that owns Straightaway Tire & Auto, commented on the strategic significance of their latest acquisitions, stating, "These acquisitions mark another step in our strategy to dominate the Minnesota market and provide top-tier customer service at all Straightaway locations. Our member companies will continue to benefit from the robust platform infrastructure aimed at accelerating their growth plans through best-in-class M&A, recruiting, training, and procurement."

---

## About Straightaway Tire & Auto

Straightaway Tire & Auto (gostraightaway.com), a portfolio company of O2 Investment Partners, has over 45 facilities across Colorado, Florida, Maine, Minnesota, New Hampshire, and Wisconsin, offering a comprehensive range of services. In addition to planned and preventive maintenance, Straightaway provides glass repair, tire sales, EV and hybrid vehicle service, and mechanical and electrical repair. The company is actively expanding its reach across the US and is pursuing partnerships with operators who are looking to accelerate growth.
