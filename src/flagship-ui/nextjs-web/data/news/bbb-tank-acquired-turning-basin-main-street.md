# BBB Tank Inc Acquired by Turning Basin Capital Partner and Main Street Capital Corp

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/bbb-tank-inc-acquired-by-turning-basin-capital-partner-and-main-street-capital-corp/

**Author:** Michael Allen

**Date:** April 11, 2016

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately-held, middle-market companies is pleased to announce the acquisition of BBB Tank, Inc. (BBB Tank), headquartered in Baytown, TX by Turning Basin Capital Partners & Main Street Capital Corp.

BBB Tank, founded in 2001, specializes in the design and construction of aboveground storage tanks, tank seals and tank repairs. Services include new tank construction, tank maintenance, repairs, and renovations, shell extensions and replacements, bottom repairs and replacements, floating roof repairs and replacements, seal systems, nozzle modifications and all related API-653 services.
