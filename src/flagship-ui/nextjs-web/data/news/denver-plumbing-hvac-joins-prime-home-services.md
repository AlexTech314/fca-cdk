# Denver Plumber and HVAC Joins the Prime Home Services Group Family

**URL:** https://www.primehomeservicesgroup.com/denver-plumber-and-hvac-joins-the-prime-home-services-group-family/

**Author:** Prime Home Services Group

**Date:** June 3, 2025

---

We are proud to announce that Denver Plumber and HVAC has officially joined Prime Home Services Group as of June 3, 2025. This exciting addition strengthens our growing network of trusted home service brands and allows us to expand our reach throughout the Denver Metro area with a company known for its exceptional service and deep community roots.

## About Denver Plumber and HVAC

Founded by Susan and William Frew, Denver Plumber and HVAC has served the Denver community and surrounding areas with high-quality plumbing and HVAC solutions for years. Built on values of integrity, customer-first service, and top-tier craftsmanship, Susan and William created a company that earned the trust of homeowners through consistent, honest, and expert work. Their legacy of excellence makes Denver Plumber and HVAC an ideal addition to the Prime family.

## A Legacy Worth Preserving

At Prime Home Services Group, our philosophy is simple: invest in great local brands, preserve what makes them special, and provide the support they need to thrive.

Denver Plumber and HVAC will continue to operate under its established and respected name, maintaining the relationships and reputation they've built in the Denver community. With the backing of Prime's resources and infrastructure, we are excited to:

- **Support Local Strength** – By maintaining Denver Plumber and HVAC's unique identity and community connections.
- **Fuel Strategic Growth** – Providing access to modern tools, advanced systems, and a network of like-minded professionals.
- **Expand Customer Benefits** – Enabling broader service capabilities and even more responsive care while keeping the same trusted team in place.

## Why This Matters for Customers

This acquisition is about elevating the customer experience. Homeowners will continue working with the same skilled technicians and office team they've come to rely on—now supported by the additional resources, technology, and operational expertise of Prime Home Services Group.
