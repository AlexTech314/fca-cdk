# Millenium Acquires Delta Fire Protection

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** June 15, 2022

Millennium is a full-service fire suppression and protection company that provides design, installation, and repair services throughout the West Coast. The acquisition of Delta provides a complementary customer base with a focus on commercial customers while expanding the company's reach in Southern California.

"Merging with Millennium will provide additional resources to enhance support to our employees and wide customer base," commented Brett Greene, Delta President.

Millennium will be moving its shop to the Delta location in North Hollywood and plans to expand the team working out of the Delta office.

"We are focused on enhancing Delta's strengths and investing in areas of growth," responded Neil Bradshaw, Millennium President & CEO. "With investment in people, systems, and processes, we are looking to significantly grow Delta within a year."

## About Millennium

Millennium was formed with the combination of Millennium Fire Protection Corporation ("MFPC") and integrated Protection Services ("IPS"). MFPC has steadily grown since its founding in 2005 into one of the most trusted full-service fire protection and life safety specialists on the West Coast with a stellar reputation among General Contractors for "value engineering" and designing, installing, and servicing complex projects. IPS focused on inspection, testing, and maintenance ("ITM") and has built a strong reputation servicing petrochemical companies. Delta will join the Millennium platform and continue to perform commercial installations and ITM in its market. Millennium is striving to be a one-stop-shop for all things related to fire protection and suppression and plans to make additional acquisitions to expand its reach and capabilities. To know more about Millenium, visit www.mfpc.us.
