# PaleoWest Adds Stratified to Further Drive Rapid Growth

**URL:** https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/paleowest-adds-stratified-to-further-drive-rapid-growth-301756812.html

**Author:** PR Newswire

**Date:** February 27, 2023

---

PaleoWest, a leading provider of cultural resource management services and a portfolio company of The Riverside Company, has acquired Stratified Environmental and Archaeological Services, LLC (SEAS), a Colorado-based cultural resource management firm.

The acquisition enhances PaleoWest's ability to simplify land development across federal, state, and local levels, while providing data that supports sustainable development and environmental compliance.

This acquisition is the second add-on investment to Riverside's portfolio company, PaleoWest.

---

## About PaleoWest

PaleoWest is a leading provider of cultural, prehistoric, architectural, ethnographic heritage and paleontological resource management services. The company helps clients navigate complex regulatory requirements while supporting sustainable development.

---

## About Stratified Environmental and Archaeological Services

Stratified Environmental and Archaeological Services, LLC (SEAS) is a Colorado-based cultural resource management firm providing environmental and archaeological consulting services.
