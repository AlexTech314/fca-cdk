# Preston Refrigeration Acquires Coldstar, Inc.

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/preston-refrigeration-acquires-coldstar-inc/

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** September 2022

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately held, middle-market companies, is pleased to announce the acquisition of Coldstar Inc. by Preston Refrigeration.

---

## About Coldstar, Inc.

Coldstar Inc. (www.coldstarusa.com), a company that specializes in the design and installation of industrial ammonia refrigeration systems, is based in Orlando, Florida. Customers all across the world have benefited from the company's high-quality industrial refrigeration equipment and services. Established in 1982 in the United Kingdom, they adhere to their company philosophies and focus on optimum energy efficiency. Coldstar Inc. also prioritizes the demands of its clients first.

---

## About Preston Refrigeration

Preston Refrigeration (www.prestonrefrigeration.com), a full-service industrial refrigeration contractor, is based in Kansas City, Missouri. The company has provided cutting-edge, effective, and dependable solutions to customers in the meat processing, food & beverage, and cold storage industries for more than 75 years. Preston now employs over 150 refrigeration specialists and offers self-performed design, build, and maintenance services all across the United States.

Preston is one of the biggest industrial refrigeration contractors in the country. The company can manage difficult projects of any size, location, and complexity because of its specialized knowledge and capabilities.
