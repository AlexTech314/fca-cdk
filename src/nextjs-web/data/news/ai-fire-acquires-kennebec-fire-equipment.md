# AI Fire Acquires Kennebec Fire Equipment

**URL:** https://mergr.com/transaction/ai-fire-acquires-kennebec-fire-equipment

**Author:** Mergr

**Date:** July 9, 2020

---

AI Fire has acquired Kennebec Fire Equipment, a supplier of fire protection equipment based in Winslow, Maine. This represents AI Fire's 6th transaction in the Safety/Security sector and its first acquisition in Maine.

## About Kennebec Fire Equipment

Kennebec Fire Equipment is a supplier of fire protection equipment based in Winslow, Maine. The company's product line includes:

- Amerex Hand Portable Fire Extinguishers
- Guardian Fire Systems
- ADX Automatic Fire Extinguishers
- Amerex Wheeled Extinguishers
- Brackets & Cabinets
- Safety Signage

**Location:** Winslow, Maine, United States

---

## About AI Fire

AI Fire provides fire and life safety services, including inspection, service, customized work orders, and installation for fire alarms, sprinklers, fire extinguishers, and other fire suppression systems. AI Fire was founded in 2009 and is based in Long Beach, California.

AI Fire is a portfolio company of Audax Private Equity.
