# American Trailer Rental Group Acquires Colorado Storage Systems

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/american-trailer-rental-group-acquires-colorado-storage-systems/

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** January 2022

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately-held, middle-market companies, is pleased to announce the acquisition of Colorado Storage Systems by American Trailer Rental Group, a Kentucky-based leading provider of trailer rental solutions to manufacturing, distribution, and third party logistics customer.

---

## About American Trailer Group

ATRG (American Trailer Rental Group, LLC) is a family of companies that offer short-term trailer rentals providing temporary or permanent warehousing space across a multitude of manufacturing and industrial customers. The Company has 19 locations spanning across the U.S. with over 16,000 storage, cartage, and over-the-road trailers. To learn more about the American Trailer Rental Group, visit www.americantrg.com.

---

## About Colorado Storage Systems

Colorado Storage Systems is a family-owned and operated company that has been in the portable storage business for over 30 years. The company's goal is to provide with the best possible service at a great price. Delivery is usually available within 24 hours with the most up-to-date security devices to secure portable storage units.
