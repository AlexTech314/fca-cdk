# APi Group Acquires Signal One Fire & Communications

**URL:** https://www.wsfp.com/who-we-are/signal-one/

**Author:** Western States Fire Protection

**Date:** June 2012

---

APi Group, Inc. has acquired Signal One Fire & Communications, a full-service fire protection partner that operates throughout the state of Arizona.

## About Signal One Fire & Communications

Signal One Fire & Communications is a full-service fire protection partner that operates throughout the state of Arizona. Signal One Fire & Communications works with fire alarm, security, CCTV, card access, and nurse call systems. The company prides itself on Quality, Integrity, Safety, Commitment and Professionalism.

Signal One Fire & Communications promptly responds to scheduled and emergency calls. Whether it is an inspection, troubleshooting or adding a device to your system, their knowledgeable technicians expertly diagnose, locate, and cost effectively work to repair problems.

### Services Offered

- Fire Protection Systems
- Fire Alarm Systems
- Fire Extinguishers
- Fire Sprinkler Systems
- Wet and Dry Chemical Fire Suppression Systems
- Clean Agents/Special Hazard Suppression Systems
- Paint Booth Systems
- Alarm Monitoring
- Corrosion Management
- Inspections
- Services & Repair
- 24 Hour Emergency Repair
- Fire Pump Rentals
- CCTV
- Security Systems
- Access Control

## Part of a Multinational Company

Signal One is a division of Western States Fire Protection which is a wholly owned subsidiary of APi Group, Inc., a multi-national company with offices across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.
