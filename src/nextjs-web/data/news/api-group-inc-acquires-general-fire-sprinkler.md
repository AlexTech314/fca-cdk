# API Group Inc. Acquires General Fire Sprinkler Company LLC

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/api-group-inc-acquires-general-fire-sprinkler/

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** January 3, 2023

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately held, middle-market companies, is pleased to announce the acquisition of General Fire Sprinkler Company, LLC by API Group, Inc.

---

## About General Fire Sprinkler Company LLC

General Fire Sprinkler Company, LLC (www.gasfpc.com) is a full-service fire protection provider that offers everything from inspections and testing to maintenance and new installations. General Fire Sprinkler has been a reputable fire protection brand in Kansas City and the neighboring areas for over 35 years. The company specializes in service and repairs on fire sprinkler systems.

---

## About API Group Inc.

API Group Inc. is a provider of safety, energy, specialty, and industrial services in over 200 locations, primarily in North America and with a growing presence in Europe. API Group serves a wide range of long-standing clients from various industries with legally required services and other contracted services. The company is headquartered in New Brighton, Minnesota, and was established in 1926.

---

## About Flatirons Capital Advisors

Flatirons Capital Advisors, LLC (www.flatironscap.com) is a Mergers and Acquisitions advisory firm that helps privately held companies sell their businesses and acquire other businesses. Our unique business model affords sell-side advisory clients the ability to improve their company's performance, earnings and effectiveness in the short-term, while simultaneously increasing their market value for a future sale. Flatirons has offices in Colorado, Florida, Illinois, and Texas.
