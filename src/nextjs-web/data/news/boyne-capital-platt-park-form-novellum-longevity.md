# Boyne Capital and Platt Park Capital Announce the Formation of Novellum Longevity

**URL:** https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/boyne-capital-and-platt-park-capital-announce-the-formation-of-novellum-longevity-302515749.html

**Author:** PR Newswire

**Date:** July 29, 2025

---

Boyne Capital and Platt Park Capital have partnered to launch Novellum Longevity as a provider of support services to longevity and functional medicine clinics.

Boyne and Platt Park's investment aims to bring longevity-focused healthcare to more communities seeking a better model for aging and wellness. Novellum will leverage acquired ThriveMD's non-clinical assets to scale hormone replacement and functional wellness services.

The company is a management services organization (MSO) overseeing the operations of longevity and functional medicine clinics.

---

## About ThriveMD

ThriveMD was a leading provider of longevity and functional medicine services that has joined with Novellum Longevity to expand its reach and capabilities.

---

## About Novellum Longevity

Novellum Longevity is a management services organization (MSO) overseeing the operations of longevity and functional medicine clinics, focused on revolutionizing healthcare with advanced wellness clinics for aging and wellness.

## About Boyne Capital

Boyne Capital is a private equity firm focused on lower middle-market investments.
