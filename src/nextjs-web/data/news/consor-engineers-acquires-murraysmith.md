# CONSOR Engineers Acquires Murraysmith and Quincy Engineering

**URL:** https://www.morrisseygoodale.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/CON_MUR-1.pdf

**Author:** Morrissey Goodale

**Date:** January 11, 2021

---

CONSOR Engineers has acquired Murraysmith and Quincy Engineering, creating a combined firm of over 1,200 professionals providing a range of planning, design, structural assessment, and construction services for transportation and water infrastructure.

## About Murraysmith

Murraysmith is a public infrastructure engineering firm headquartered in Portland, Oregon. The firm specializes in delivering high-quality water and transportation infrastructure projects for public agency clients in the western United States.

Murray, Smith & Associates, Inc. (MSA) is a full-service civil and environmental consulting engineering firm with offices in Oregon, Washington and Idaho.

---

## About CONSOR Engineers

CONSOR Engineers is a national engineering firm providing planning, design, structural assessment, and construction services. The company has grown through significant acquisitions including Structural Grace, TKW Consulting Engineers, U.S. Underwater, Apex Design, and now Murraysmith and Quincy Engineering.

---

## About the Merger

In May 2019, public infrastructure engineering firm Murraysmith (Portland, OR) merged with Quincy Engineering, which specializes in transportation engineering. This combined entity was then acquired by CONSOR Engineers in January 2021.

Together, the combined 1,200+ professionals provide a range of planning, design, structural assessment, and construction services for transportation and water infrastructure projects.
