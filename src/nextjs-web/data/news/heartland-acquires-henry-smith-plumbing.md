# Heartland Home Services Acquires Henry Smith Plumbing, Heating & Cooling

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/heartland-home-services-acquires-henry-smith-plumbing-heating-cooling/

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** December 17, 2021

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately-held, middle-market companies, is pleased to announce the acquisition of business services company Henry Smith Plumbing, Heating & Cooling, headquartered in Elkhart, Indiana, by Heartland Home Services.

---

## About Heartland Home Services

Heartland Home Services is the leading provider of non-discretionary, residential HVAC replacement and maintenance, plumbing, and electrical services in the Midwest. The company currently maintains nine brands operating across five states. To learn more about Heartland Home Services, visit www.heartlandhomeservices.com.

---

## About Henry Smith Plumbing, Heating & Cooling

Henry Smith is a provider of residential or commercial water heater, air quality, plumbing, heating repair, or cooling services. Henry Smith was founded in 1952 by Henry Smith himself. For more than 60 years, the company has held to high standards of customer care. To learn more about Henry Smith Plumbing, Heating & Cooling, visit www.henrysmithphc.com.
