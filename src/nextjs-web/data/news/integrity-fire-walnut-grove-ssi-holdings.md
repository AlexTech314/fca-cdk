# Integrity Fire & Walnut Grove Capital Join with State Systems to Form SSI Fire & Safety Holdings

**URL:** Company History

**Author:** Integrity Fire Safety Services

**Date:** November 2021

---

Integrity Fire Safety Services and Walnut Grove Capital have joined with State Systems to form SSI Fire & Safety Holdings, LLC, creating a leading fire and life safety platform in Colorado.

## About Integrity Fire Safety Services

Integrity Fire Safety Services was founded in February 2018 by eleven fire and life safety experts with a shared vision: to revolutionize fire protection in Colorado. As veterans of the industry, they came together to offer a forward-thinking approach, driven by a passion for protecting life and property.

From a small team navigating uncharted waters to a thriving company with over 150 fire and life safety professionals, Integrity Fire's journey is a testament to resilience and vision.
