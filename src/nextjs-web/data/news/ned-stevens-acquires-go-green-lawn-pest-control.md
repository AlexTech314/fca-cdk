# Ned Stevens Announces Strategic Expansion into Lawn Care with Go Green Lawn and Pest Control

**URL:** https://nedstevens.com/go-green-press-release/

**Author:** Ned Stevens

**Date:** 2024

---

Ned Stevens Gutter Cleaning ("Ned Stevens"), a leading provider of gutter cleaning and exterior home services in the United States, is excited to announce its service expansion into lawn fertilization and pest control through a strategic partnership with Go Green Lawn and Pest Control ("Go Green"). This partnership allows Ned Stevens to begin offering premium lawn fertilization services to its loyal customers in Pennsylvania, Maryland, Delaware, and Southern New Jersey. Simultaneously, it gives Go Green's customers access to nearly six decades of expertise in gutter and exterior home cleaning.

Headquartered in West Chester, PA., Go Green provides lawn fertilization and pest control services in Pennsylvania, Maryland, Delaware, and Southern New Jersey. Go Green was founded by Andrew Gabries in 2011, with the goal of creating green landscapes while preserving the sustainability of their hometown community.

Through this partnership, Andrew and his team will bring their lawn care expertise to the Ned Stevens family, contributing to the goal of delivering high-quality lawn care services in all Ned Stevens' markets.

"My team and I are looking forward to the growth and opportunity this partnership will provide. We believe the partnership with Ned is a great fit not only for our current customers, but also for our team," said founder and CEO, Andrew Gabries. "Go Green has always been focused on growth and now we will be able to expand the services we offer our customers and extend the resources available to our team members."

"When looking for the right lawn care business to partner with, we searched all over the country, and Go Green was the perfect fit for us based on their strong team, solid infrastructure, subscription model, and their belief in customer happiness and family culture," added Rob Rapuano, CEO, Ned Stevens.

## About Ned Stevens

Ned Stevens is one of the country's fastest growing and most trusted gutter cleaning and home maintenance companies. Since its founding in 1965 in Fairfield, New Jersey, Ned Stevens has expanded its service offerings and geographic footprint, servicing 17 states including Washington D.C.
