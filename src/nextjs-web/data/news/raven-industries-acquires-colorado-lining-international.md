# Raven Industries (NASDAQ:RAVN) Acquires Colorado Lining International Inc

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/raven-industries-nasdaqravn-acquires-colorado-lining-international-inc/

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** September 10, 2017

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately-held, middle-market companies is pleased to announce the acquisition of Colorado Lining International, Inc. (CLI) by Raven Industries (NASDAQ:RAVN) headquartered in Sioux Falls, SD.

Founded in 1978, CLI operates a full service Geosynthetics Contractor offering factory expert geomembrane fabrication and installation under one roof. CLI offers assistance in flexible membrane liner selection, coating material selection, fabrication techniques and design/build installation.

Keith Wegen, President of Flatirons, led the deal team that advised CLI on the transaction. "CLI's longevity, multiple locations, diversification, solid management team, and high profile client base make it a great fit for Raven," stated Wegen.

## About Raven Industries

Raven Industries (NASDAQ:RAVN) is dedicated to providing innovative, high-value products and solutions that solve great challenges throughout the world. Raven is a leader in precision agriculture, high-performance specialty films, and lighter-than-air technologies. Since 1956, Raven has designed, produced, and delivered exceptional solutions, earning the company a reputation for innovation, product quality, high performance, and unmatched service.
