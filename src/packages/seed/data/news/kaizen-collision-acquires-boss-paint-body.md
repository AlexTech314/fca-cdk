# Kaizen Collision Center Acquires Boss Paint and Body

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/kaizen-collision-center/

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** August 30, 2021

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately-held, middle-market companies, is pleased to announce the acquisition of Boss Paint and Body by Kaizen Collision Center, a provider of collision repair services in Arizona, Colorado, and California.

---

## About Kaizen Collision Center

Kaizen Collision Center has been serving Arizona's automotive needs for years. The meaning of the word Kaizen is "to continuously improve", which is a daily goal within the company. They provide the best auto repair services in each area they serve. To learn more about Kaizen Collision Center, visit www.kaizenautocare.com.

---

## About Boss Paint and Body

Boss Paint and Body is a local auto body shop. The company specializes in a wide range of services including collision repair, insurance claim handling, bodywork, frame straightening, refurbishing, enhancements, painting, precision color match, rental cars, window replacement, and miscellaneous repairs. To know more about Boss Paint and Body, visit www.bosspaintandbody.com.

## About Flatirons Capital Advisors

Flatirons Capital Advisors, LLC (www.flatironscap.com) is a Mergers and Acquisitions advisory firm that helps privately held companies sell their businesses and acquire other businesses. Our unique business model affords sell-side advisory clients the ability to improve their company's performance, earnings, and effectiveness in the short-term, while simultaneously increasing their market value for a future sale. Flatirons has offices in Colorado, Florida, Illinois, and Texas.
