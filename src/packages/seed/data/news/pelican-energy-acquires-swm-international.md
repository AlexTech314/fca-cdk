# Pelican Energy Partners Acquires SWM International, Inc.

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/pelican-energy-partners-acquires-swm-international-inc/

**Author:** Michael Allen

**Date:** September 2019

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately-held, middle-market companies, is pleased to announce the acquisition of SWM International, Inc. by Pelican Energy Partners.

SWM International is one of the largest, independent manufacturers of perforating guns and downhole tools. Flatirons Capital Advisors was the exclusive sell-side advisor for SWM International, Inc.

---

## About SWM International

SWM International is a leading designer and manufacturer of perforating tools and downhole tools for the oil and gas industry. Headquartered in Pampa, TX, SWM provides essential equipment for well completion and production operations.

---

## About Pelican Energy Partners

Pelican Energy Partners is a Houston-based private equity firm focused on investments in the energy services sector. Pelican partners with management teams to build leading energy services companies.
