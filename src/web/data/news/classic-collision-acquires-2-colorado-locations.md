# Classic Collision Acquires 2 Colorado Locations

**URL:** https://flatironscap.com/classic-collision-acquires-2-colorado-locations/

**Author:** Keith Wegen

**Date:** August 26, 2024

---

Flatirons Capital Advisors, a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately held, middle-market companies, is pleased to announce the acquisition of Precision Auto Works by Classic Collision, a portfolio company of TPG.

Classic Collision, LLC, a leading national multi-site collision repair operator based in Atlanta, acquires two new locations in Colorado, Precision Auto Works in Bloomfield and Williams Body & Paint in Colorado Springs.

Precision Auto Works, a family-owned business operating for thirty years, has proudly served the Denver Front Range community. Known as a trusted and reliable destination for automotive services, the team was committed to meeting every customer's needs. "We have been passionate about providing top-notch services to our valued customers, and we are honored to be part of the Classic Collision organization now," said Rod Brock, former owner of Precision Auto Works.

Williams Body & Paint, a locally-owned full-service auto body shop in Colorado Springs, has provided efficient and affordable collision repairs for twenty years. "Over the past decade, we have honed our business to provide the most efficient, high-quality collision repair in the Colorado Springs area. We are excited to join the Classic Collision team and continue our efforts," stated Jerome Williams, former owner of Williams Body & Paint.

## About Classic Collision

Founded in 1983 in Atlanta, Georgia, Classic Collision set out with a clear mission: to provide exceptional service with integrity and honesty. They achieve this by hiring top talent and utilizing the finest materials. Today, Classic Collision is one of the largest privately owned auto body repair service providers nationally. For more than 40 years, customer satisfaction has been at the forefront of everything we do. We take pride in delivering high-quality auto body repairs through skilled technicians and state-of-the-art equipment. With numerous manufacturer certifications and factory-direct training, we have established ourselves as a leader in comprehensive collision repair.
