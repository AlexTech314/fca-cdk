## Excellence is our foundation.

Flatirons Capital Advisors is a leading mergers and acquisitions advisor to privately-held, middle-market companies. With decades of transaction advisory experience and entrepreneurship, our founders identified a growing need in the middle-market business community to bring together a more comprehensive suite of professional resources that combines transaction expertise with the transformational power of experienced business advisory professionals. The deal process is 100% managed by a senior team member and not pushed down to a junior analyst. This hands-on approach by senior team members ensures a strategic and robust process for our clients.

## Leadership Team

### R. Michael Allen
**CEO and Founder**

As CEO and Founder, Mr. Allen leads our Business Development Group and assesses potential buyer interest in our firm's prospective sell-side opportunities. This is facilitated through a close working relationship with our Transactions Group and an exceptional ability to analyze the true value of a company from a buyer's perspective. With more than 25 years of M&A experience, Mr. Allen has been directly involved in the execution of more than 400 client transactions, including mergers, acquisitions, recapitalizations, and opinions of value in some of the most volatile industries, including technology, pharmaceutical, aerospace, manufacturing, distribution, and energy.

Previously in his career, Mr. Allen was a Managing Director of McGladrey Capital Markets, a subsidiary of H&R Block. Mr. Allen also served as Managing Partner of DTF Management, a private equity group specializing in technology-based companies (Diversified Technology Fund I, II, & III). Prior positions include Managing Director of Intercontinental Equities, a NASD (FINRA) registered broker-dealer, where he held Series 22 and 63 securities licenses.

As an entrepreneur, Mr. Allen founded and operated three companies in the consulting, distribution and technology sectors. Mr. Allen was CEO of Bitfield Technologies, LLC, which was the parent company of Bitfield, OY based in Helsinki, Finland. Bitfield Technologies was a leading developer of hardware & software for the videoconferencing arena. Mr. Allen acquired and recapitalized the company, implementing a complete turnaround of the enterprise, and then sold it to a public European company named Algo Vision, Plc.

Mr. Allen attended the University of Colorado and earned his postgraduate Business Management Certification at University of Colorado.

### Keith Wegen
**President and Founder**

As President and Founder, Mr. Wegen's principal activity is to direct the firm's M&A activities. He oversees the team that guides the firm's clients from engagement through the closing of a transaction. Mr. Wegen and his team are responsible for preparing clients to go to market, guiding activity throughout the sales process, facilitating meetings, and managing the due diligence and legal process, all the while protecting our clients' interests to assure the successful completion of the sale process and the client's transition from the business.

Mr. Wegen began his career in Connecticut as a financial analyst with GE Capital Corporation and served in various financial capacities with GE subsidiaries. Mr. Wegen later moved to Colorado and joined Ventura, Inc., an investment and business consulting firm, where he was responsible for originating deal flow and evaluating the technical, financial, market, and management risks associated with new ventures.

Prior to joining Flatirons Capital Advisors, Mr. Wegen started Provident Investment Partners, LLC with another partner and invested client funds in the stock market. As an Investment Advisor, Mr. Wegen held a FINRA (NASD) Series 65 securities license.

Mr. Wegen earned his B.S. in Finance from the University of Vermont in Burlington, Vermont.

## Community Service

The entire team at Flatirons Capital Advisors loves giving back to the community:

- **Rippling Waters Kego:** Providing sustainable education, food, and water for the orphaned children of the Lake Victoria area impacted by the AIDS epidemic.

- **Community Food Share:** Community Food Share's mission is to be a leader in our community's effort to end hunger in Boulder and Broomfield Counties. The Flatirons Capital Advisors team works on the floor of the distribution center very often on a weekly basis.

- **Project Healing Waters:** Dedicated to the physical and emotional rehabilitation of disabled active military service personnel and disabled veterans through fly fishing and associated activities including education, outings, fly tying, rod building, fly casting and knot tying. The Flatirons Capital Advisors team has participated in fund raising efforts and has taken multiple veterans out on guided fly fishing trips.

- **Skate for Prostate:** Keith Wegen created this program, raising tens of thousands of dollars for non-profits that focus on prostate cancer awareness.

- **Wounded Warrior Project:** Flatirons Capital Advisors has participated in more than 14 Tough Mudders and led teams in fundraising efforts for the Wounded Warrior Project.
